Lizy Gershenzon is an accomplished product designer with nearly 20 years of experience designing online platforms, directing marketing campaigns, and building businesses. As a co-founder and owner of Future Fonts and Vectro Type, she has been instrumental in the development of these innovative type companies. In addition to her work with these companies, Lizy has also been a partner at Scribble Tone for the past 15 years, and consults with leading product design teams.
