Lâm Bảo co-founded Yellow Type Foundry with Duy Đào in Vietnam in 2018, alongside being an independent designer focusing on product & visual design. During the years, they have worked with a couple of renown Vietnamese brands, including OPPO Vietnam, beGroup, mCredit by MBBank, and a few more to be named or in progress. The duo focus on delivering the best of their craftmanship in modern typefaces, especially made for Vietnamese and continue contributing to the next generation of type designers in Vietnam.
